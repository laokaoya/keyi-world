The person behind the résumé简历背后的人

Spin-½ particles never point in one direction. Neither do I — and I've stopped pretending that's a problem. 自旋½的粒子从不固定方向。我也是——已经不再假装这是个问题了。

My Xiaohongshu handle is 半自旋 (spin-half) — named after particles that can't commit to a single axis. Feels accurate. I started at PKU in geochemistry (China's most notorious "career sinkhole"), escaped into economics, then AI. Somewhere in between I spent a birthday on an Italian glacier staring at 4.5 billion years of exposed rock and thought: geological timescales are genuinely therapeutic. Best thinking happens between 2 and 4am. This is a feature. 小红书名叫"半自旋"——量子力学里那种不能固定轴向的粒子。感觉很贴切。本科在北大读了地球化学（传说中的天坑之王），后来转经济，又转了AI。中间有一年在意大利阿尔卑斯的冰川上过生日，对着45亿年的岩层发了很久的呆——这种尺度感对焦虑很有帮助。我的最佳思维时间是凌晨两点到四点。这是feature。
